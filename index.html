Profile

Bridging Aerosol Science, Sensors & AI

I am a PhD researcher at the Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar studying Aerosol Science, with an extensive focus on its applications in environmental engineering, including air-pollution monitoring, spatio-temporal modelling, and the deployment of low-cost IoT sensor networks.

I am highly skilled in developing advanced machine learning and deep learning models (GNN, LSTM, CNN) for PM2.5 prediction, dynamic forecasting, and integrating high-resolution air quality data with meteorological features to generate impactful, research-driven insights that support environmental decision-making.
