Projects & Focus Areas

Exploring the intersection of atmospheric science, environmental engineering, and artificial intelligence to tackle real-world air quality challenges.

🔬 Primary Research Project

Environmental Quality Monitoring System

Urban Air Quality Monitoring Using Low-Cost IoT Sensors

Developing a comprehensive environmental monitoring system that integrates low-cost sensors for real-time air quality assessment across urban environments. This project combines hardware design, sensor calibration, data processing, and advanced ML/DL models for pollution forecasting.

Key Measurements

  • ✓ PM₁, PM₂.₅, PM₁₀ (Particulate Matter)
  • ✓ CO₂, CO (Gaseous Pollutants)
  • ✓ TVOCs (Volatile Organic Compounds)
  • ✓ Meteorological: Temp, Humidity, Pressure

Technologies & Methods

  • ✓ IoT Sensor Networks (Dense Deployment)
  • ✓ CNN-BiLSTM for Anomaly Detection
  • ✓ Spatio-Temporal Forecasting
  • ✓ Real-time Data Analytics & Visualization

Research Outcomes

  • Dense sensor network deployed across urban areas with real-time server-based data integration
  • 24-hour, 7-day, and 1-month pollution forecasting capabilities with high accuracy
  • Policy-ready insights for urban air quality management and public health decision-making

Research Focus Areas

📡

IoT Sensor Networks

Design, deployment, and real-time data integration of affordable sensor nodes. Focus on calibration accuracy and long-term sensor stability.

🤖

AI/ML Calibration

Machine-learning pipelines for field calibration of low-cost sensors against reference monitors. Sensor fusion and data quality assurance.

🧠

Spatio-Temporal Modelling

Graph neural networks and transformer architectures for high-resolution pollutant forecasting. Meteorological feature integration and dynamics modeling.

📊

Health Risk Assessment

Quantifying exposure-response relationships and health impacts from air pollution. Stochastic and deterministic risk modeling.

🌍

Ambient Air Quality

Ambient pollution characterization, source apportionment, and seasonal/episodic analysis. Urban background and traffic-influenced sites.

🏢

Indoor Air Quality

Indoor source identification, ventilation assessment, and occupant exposure estimation. Building energy efficiency vs. air quality trade-offs.
